Chocolate Cheesecake



This recipe will result in a luxuriously rich chocolate cheesecake. No baking required. Not even any gelatine required. All chocolate and dairy goodness!


INGREDIENTS

The Base

  • Oreo cookies (190g)
  • Butter - melted (45g)

The Chocolate Cheesecake

  • Dark chocolate (230g)
  • Cream cheese (400g)
  • Icing sugar (80g)
  • Vanilla extract (1 tspn)
  • Pinch of salt
  • Double/whipping cream (125ml)

The Chocolate Ganache

  • Dark chocolate (85g)
  • Double/whipping cream (110ml)

The Garnish

  • Raspberries

INSTRUCTIONS

    The Base

  1. Using a food processor, blend the oreo cookies into fine crumbs. Pour into a bowl together with melted butter. Mix well and pour into a 7 inch (18 cm) springform pan. Press tightly and evenly to the base. Place in the freezer for 30 minutes.

    2. The Chocolate Cheesecake

  2. Melt chocolate and set aside to slightly cool before using.
  3. In a bowl, put in cream cheese and whisk for a few minutes until light and fluffy. Then add in icing sugar and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.
  4. While mixing, pour in the melted chocolate into the cream cheese mixture. Continue to mix until everything is well combined. Set aside.
  5. In another bowl, whisk whipping cream until soft peaks. Then pour it into the chocolate cream cheese mixture and gently fold using a spatula until everything it well combined. Pour into the base and spread evenly. Chill in the fridge overnight or until fully set.

    6. The Chocolate Ganache

  6. Heat whipping cream until it's about to boil and then pour it into a bowl containing the chopped chocolate. Let it sit for 3 minutes then gently whisk until everything it combined.
  7. Pour onto the cheesecake and swirl around to help cover the surface. Chill in the fridge for 1 hour or until the chocolate sets. Remove cheesecake from the pan, decorate with some raspberries, slice and enjoy!
